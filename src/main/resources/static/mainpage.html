Hi, welcome to Satellite Mapping! As growing numbers of satellites and space debris crowd Low-Earth Orbit(LEO), they face an increasing risk of collision. In September 2020, the manned International Space Station (ISS) faced a near-miss event when unknown space debris passed by the Station, missing it by only a few kilometers (Read more here). My website uses NORAD TLE data to predict satellite speed and location to forecast potential impending collisions. Please visit the ‘Background information’ tab for useful resources, detailed summaries, and important formulae. Thank you for visiting!
